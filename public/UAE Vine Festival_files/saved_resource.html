how it works

Running from the 21st March - 8th April, the UAE Vine Festival is a three-week celebration of grapes in all shapes, form and glory, showcasing the best wine, food and hospitality on offer across the United Arab Emirates. 

With 40+ experiences to choose from wine lovers are assured to be dazzled by some of the best names in hospitality. The festival combines an array of curated events (blind tastings, pairing dinners, themed experiences + more) crafted by our Sommeliers and each venue's Head Chef, bringing delight to your taste buds.

Grab your friends, find your favourite event and secure your tickets before they sell out!

Grape in the age of covid

As much as we love the world of grapes, nothing is more important to us than your safety.

 

All of our events adhere to the UAE guidelines for Covid Safety, including a maximum of eight people per table, a clear gap of two meters between each, clear marking and signage, and physical distancing to avoid crowding. Masks and social distancing are also a must whilst moving around.

By doing this we can all enjoy the festival and discover a world of grape without ever leaving the country.
